We are a premium quality craft brewery in Monroe Washington serving local establishments in the surrounding area.

Lucky 7 The Irish Red

Lucky 7 The Irish Red is a very smooth and easy drinking ale with a malty flavor and aroma and just a touch of Black Barley for a beautiful red color and adding a slightly toasty finish. A real nod to the true Irish Red style ale.
